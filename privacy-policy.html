一、我们收集哪些信息

简短回答：无。

SplitCam 不会收集、存储或传输任何个人身份信息。具体而言：

  • 不收集姓名、邮箱、电话号码等身份信息
  • 不收集设备标识符（IDFA、IDFV 等）
  • 不收集位置信息
  • 不收集使用行为数据或分析数据
  • 不包含任何第三方分析 SDK（如 Firebase、友盟等）
  • 不包含任何广告 SDK

二、设备权限使用说明

本应用需要以下设备权限才能正常运行，所有权限仅用于应用核心功能：

📷 相机权限

用于前后双摄同时拍摄照片和录制视频。所有拍摄内容仅在设备本地处理，不会上传至任何服务器。

🎙 麦克风权限

用于录制视频时同步采集音频。音频数据仅保存在本地视频文件中。

🖼 相册权限

用于将拍摄的照片和视频保存到您的系统相册，以及在合拍模式下从相册导入视频。我们不会读取或访问您相册中的其他内容。

三、照片和视频数据

您使用 SplitCam 拍摄的所有照片和视频：

Overview

SplitCam (hereinafter referred to as "the App") is a dual-camera split-screen camera application developed and operated by an independent developer. We understand the importance of personal privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the App.

Core Commitment: SplitCam does not collect, upload, or share any of your personal data. Your photos and videos are stored only on your local device.

Effective Date: April 1, 2025

Last Updated: April 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Short Answer: None.

SplitCam does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. Specifically:

  • No collection of names, emails, phone numbers, or other identity information
  • No collection of device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, etc.)
  • No collection of location data
  • No collection of usage behavior or analytics data
  • No third-party analytics SDKs (such as Firebase, Google Analytics, etc.)
  • No advertising SDKs

2. Device Permissions

The App requires the following device permissions to function properly. All permissions are used solely for core app features:

📷 Camera Access

Used for simultaneously capturing photos and recording videos with both front and rear cameras. All captured content is processed locally on your device and is never uploaded to any server.

🎙 Microphone Access

Used for recording audio during video capture. Audio data is stored only in local video files.

🖼 Photo Library Access

Used to save captured photos and videos to your system photo library, and to import videos in Duet mode. We do not read or access any other content in your photo library.

3. Photo and Video Data

All photos and videos captured using SplitCam:

  • Are stored only in your device's local photo library
  • Are never uploaded to any cloud server
  • Are not used by the App for any other purpose
  • Are entirely under your control and management

4. Subscriptions and Purchases

SplitCam offers in-app subscriptions (Pro version) to unlock advanced features. Regarding subscriptions:

  • All payments are processed through Apple's App Store. We cannot access your payment information (credit card numbers, Apple ID passwords, etc.)
  • Subscription status is managed by Apple's StoreKit framework
  • You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions
  • After the free trial period ends, the subscription will auto-renew unless you cancel before the trial period ends

5. Third-Party Services

SplitCam does not integrate any third-party data collection services, including but not limited to:

  • Advertising platforms (no ads)
  • Analytics tools (no tracking)
  • Social media SDKs
  • Crash reporting services (only Apple's built-in crash reporting is used)

6. Data Security

Since SplitCam does not collect or transmit any user data, there is no risk of data breaches. All captured content is processed and stored locally on your device, protected by iOS system-level security.

7. Children's Privacy

SplitCam does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Since the App does not collect any user data, it is suitable for users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we modify this Privacy Policy, we will post the updated version on this page and update the "Last Updated" date. We recommend that you review this policy periodically to stay informed.
